Lee Hsien Loong Environmental Talk

Building on the success of our "Green Talk Conversation" in 2021, we are thrilled to announce our plans to expand our audience and reach an even broader community. Our commitment to environmental awareness and sustainability has led us to engage with East Coast GRC homeowners, bringing the conversation closer to home.

During the talk, Mr. Lee will discuss various environmental initiatives and projects he has been involved in, sharing his vision for the future of East Coast GRC. Learn about ongoing tree-planting campaigns, clean-up events, and plans to create more green spaces in our community. Be prepared to be inspired to take action in your own life and join the movement for a more sustainable future.
